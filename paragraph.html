Fall is my favorite season because its when my birthday is and its moderately chilly weather greatly appeals to me.

Birthday is the most special day for me and celebrating it with my family while I’m all dressed in jackets or cozy sweatshirts and enjoying hot cocoa at a restaurant is delightful.

Even more, I despise a super hot or super cold weather, but fall falls nearly in the middle, leaning a bit more towards the cold, but I like that.

The most beautiful part of it is watching the vibrant leaves of orange, red, brown, and green surrounding you and showering on you or your car wherever you voyage.

I love wearing jackets, sweaters, boots, and beeanies that become the fashion when fall arrives.

I enjoy celebrating halloween with my younger siblings and friends too because we get to eat so many candies.

Fall also marks the start of the football season at my school, giving me and my friends the opportunity to watch the football game together out in the field.

Fall’s chilly weather relieves people of the scorching hot weather of summer and offers a smooth transition into winter.

These are all the reasons why I enjoy fall.
